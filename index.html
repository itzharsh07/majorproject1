History of Indian Politics

India gained independence in 1947 and adopted its constitution in 1950, establishing a parliamentary system of government.

Major political transformations occurred during the Nehruvian era, Emergency period, Liberalization in the 1990s, and the rise of regional parties.

Major Political Parties

  • Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
  • Indian National Congress (INC)
  • Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)
  • Trinamool Congress (TMC)
  • Communist Party of India (CPI)

Current Political Landscape

As of 2025, BJP leads the central government with Narendra Modi as the Prime Minister.

Opposition parties are strategizing for upcoming general elections amid rising debates on economy, education, and national security.
